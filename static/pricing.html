Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Product and Services pricing?

+

Product pricing gives you self-service access to our workflow library (DIY approach). Services pricing is for direct work with our automation specialists (Done-With-You or Done-For-You approach). Many customers start with Product plans and add Services as needed.

Can I switch plans later?

+

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your Product plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the difference. Services can be purchased individually or as bundles anytime.

Do you offer refunds?

+

Product subscriptions: 14-day money-back guarantee. Services: Satisfaction guaranteed - we'll work with you until you're happy with the results or provide a full refund within 30 days of service delivery.

What integrations do workflows support?

+

Our workflows support 200+ integrations including Gmail, Slack, Airtable, Salesforce, HubSpot, Notion, Stripe, and many more. Free tier includes basic integrations. Advanced integrations available in Pro and Business plans.

How do Expert Consultations work?

+

Book a session through our calendar, share your challenge beforehand, and join a video call with our automation specialist. You'll receive actionable recommendations and follow-up notes. Sessions are typically 60 minutes at $150/hour.

What's included in the Monthly Retainer?

+

20 hours per month of automation work, priority support channel, monthly strategy calls, and rollover hours (up to 10 hours). Perfect for businesses needing ongoing automation support without hiring full-time.

Can I combine Product plans with Services?

+

Absolutely! This is our recommended approach. Start with a Product plan for workflow access, then add Services when you need expert help with implementation, customization, or training. Business plan subscribers receive 10% discount on all Services.

Do you provide technical support?

+

Yes! Support level varies by plan: Free (community forum), Starter (email support), Pro (chat + email), Business (SLA with 4-hour response). All Services include direct support during the engagement.
